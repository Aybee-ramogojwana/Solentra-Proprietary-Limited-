The Challenge & Our Solution

Botswana faces significant energy challenges that impact both households and businesses. We're here to change that.

The Problem

Load-shedding Crisis

Businesses lose approximately BWP 10 million per day due to power outages across the country.

High Electricity Costs

The average household spends BWP 12,000 annually on electricity, with rates continuing to rise.

Limited Access

Rural communities struggle to find reliable solar vendors and installation services.

Daily Economic Impact BWP 10M

Reliable Power

Cost Savings
